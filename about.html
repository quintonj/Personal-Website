About Me

Born and raised in Atlanta, I attended the Georgia Institute of Technology where I graduated in 2018 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Industrial Design. I am currently working at The Home Depot as a User Experience Designer within their Enterprise UX Team. My distinctive background has given me the opportunity to work both my left and right brain. As a designer, it is my goal to create compelling, unique, and thoughtful experiences that are feasible.
